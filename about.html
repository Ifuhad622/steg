Our History

The Sierra Leone National Shipping Agency (SLNSA) was established to provide comprehensive maritime services for government, private sectors, and international trade. Our transformation reflects our commitment to efficiency, transparency, and professionalism.

Mission

To deliver high-quality shipping and logistics services to our clients while adhering to global standards and promoting maritime safety.

Vision

To be the leading shipping agency in West Africa, recognized for excellence, integrity, and innovation.
